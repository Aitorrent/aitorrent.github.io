I am currently a Reseach associate in the Deparment of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences under the supervision of Prof. Isztar Zawadzki. My dissertation was entitled: "Integration of NWP, hydrological models and radar-based nowcasting for flood forecasting in real time." [pdf in spanish].
